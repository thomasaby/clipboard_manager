ClipSuit Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

ClipSuit is a clipboard manager Chrome extension that stores all user data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your data on any server or third-party service.

2. Data We Collect

ClipSuit does not collect any personal or clipboard data. All copied items are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API.

3. How Data is Stored

Your clipboard history is stored securely in your browser and cannot be accessed by us or other users.

4. Third-Party Access

We do not share your clipboard data with any third party. All data remains local to your device.

5. Security

While the data is local, please note that anyone with access to your device can access the stored clipboard history.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us via our GitHub repository: thomasaby/clipboard_manager.
